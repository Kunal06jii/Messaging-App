Our Commitment to Privacy

At ChitChat, we are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring your conversations remain private. We use state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols to safeguard your information.

1. End-to-End Encryption

All messages, calls, and media shared on ChitChat are protected with end-to-end encryption, which means only you and the recipient can read or listen to them.

2. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers. Your information stays with you.

3. Data Collection

ChitChat collects only minimal information necessary to provide you with secure messaging services. This includes your account ID and technical logs for troubleshooting.

4. User Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete chats, remove media, or deactivate your account at any time.

5. Continuous Security

Our team continuously monitors and updates security features to protect you against new threats and vulnerabilities.
